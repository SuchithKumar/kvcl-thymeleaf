Karnataka Vasavi Sports League is organized by Vasavi Yuva Jana Sanga, Malleswaram & Vasavi Youth Centre since 8 years Every year we organize State Level Cricket, Badminton & Carrom competition. The Motto is “Sports for Unity” and the organization is actively running under the leadership of R.P.Ravishankar (President of Karnataka Arya Vysya Mahasabha) & Sri P.N.Govindarajulu (President of Malleswaram Arya Vysya Sanga). This year the KCVL is organized by Vasavi Yuvajana Sangha Bangalore
